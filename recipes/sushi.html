California-Style Sushi Rolls

Description

Soft, vinegared rice wrapped around silky avocado, crisp cucumber, and salmon (or crab) for a classic California-style roll. A sprinkle of toasted sesame seeds adds aroma and a gentle crunch, while pickled ginger and soy sauce round out the bite.

This approachable recipe yields 4 rolls (about 24 pieces) and is perfect for a weeknight sushi night. You can swap in your favorite fillings or keep it vegetarian with extra veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice, rinsed
  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 avocado, thinly sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • 4 oz smoked salmon or imitation crab
  • 4 tbsp cream cheese (optional)
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Soy sauce & pickled ginger, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook rice with the water until tender. Combine rice vinegar, sugar, and salt; fold into hot rice and let cool slightly.
  2. Prep fillings: slice avocado and cucumber; cut salmon/crab into strips; soften cream cheese.
  3. Set a bamboo mat with plastic wrap. Place 1 sheet nori shiny-side down and spread a thin, even layer of rice over it. Sprinkle sesame seeds.
  4. Flip the sheet so the rice faces down. Arrange avocado, cucumber, salmon/crab, and cream cheese in a line along the bottom third.
  5. Roll tightly using the mat, pressing to seal the edge.

  7. Serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger.
